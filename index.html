Susan Hargus About Me

I moved to Iowa from Los Angles about 15 years ago with my husband to raise our two boys in the midwest.

I have a BFA from U of Iowa and I also attended Minneapolis College of Art and Design and the Atelier, where I learned to
paint portraits in the style of classical realism.
